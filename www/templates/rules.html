Rule book

Story

You inherited this island and is now trying to develop a civilization that can support a space program so that you can get to Moon.

Building costs

At the bottom left corner you see amount of resources that you got at the moment. A greyed out build button means that you don't have enough resources

Additionally there may be at most 5 towns and 4 cities at the board at any time. A city does not count as a town here. So you can upgrade a town to free up, so that you can build another town.

Dice

At the beginning of your turn, the dice is rolled. Houses that have resources with the sum of the two dices, will get 1 resource. If you have a city, you get 2 pieces of resource.

If you roll a total of 7, and have more than 4 resources, all but 4 resources will be robbed.

Space program

In order to get to Moon, you need to get {{numSpaceShuttlePartsTarget}} parts to your space shuttle. Rumors say that development cards is a good source for space shuttle parts.
